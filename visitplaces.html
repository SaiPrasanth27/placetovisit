Srikalahasti

Srikalahasti is a famous temple town located 36 km from Tirupati. Known for its ancient Shiva temple, it is considered a must-visit destination.

Chandragiri Fort

This historical fort is located 16 km from Tirupati and showcases exquisite architecture from the Vijayanagara Empire.

Kapila Theertham

A sacred waterfall and temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located at the base of the Tirumala hills.
